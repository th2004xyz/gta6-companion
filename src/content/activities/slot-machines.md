## 概述

老虎机（Slot Machines）是全球赌场最普及的随机类玩法，规则极简、节奏快、单次投入低，是赌场生态中受众最广的入门组件。在《Grand Theft Auto VI》回归 Vice City 与拓展 Leonida 州赌场场景的背景下，老虎机作为赌场玩法矩阵的基石组件，回归几乎确定。本条目标记为 speculated，依据为 Rockstar 虽已在《GTA Online》Diamond Casino & Resort 更新中实现了完整的老虎机系统，但尚未在 GTA6 的预告片或新闻稿中明确展示该玩法画面。

## 系列历史

老虎机在 GTA 系列中的实现可追溯至《GTA Online》的赌场更新，其玩法框架已经过多年沉淀：

- **《GTA Online》Diamond Casino & Resort 更新（2019-07）**：老虎机作为三款核心玩法之一首次加入，与轮盘、21 点共同构成赌场玩法矩阵
- **主题多样性**：提供多款主题机型，每款拥有独立的符号集与赔付表，如 Diamond Miner、Evil Tree、Impotent Rage 等，呼应 Rockstar 自有 IP
- **投注模式**：支持 1–5 条赔付线的选择，玩家可按风险偏好调整单次投注金额
- **赔付结构**：标准 5×3 滚轮布局，赔付依据特定符号组合在赔付线上的命中数量计算
- **Jackpot 机制**：特定主题机型设有累计 Jackpot，全服玩家持续贡献奖池，中奖者获得大额赔付
- **辅助功能**：提供"快速旋转"模式，加快节奏以适应高频玩家

GTA6 在此基础上的延续几乎是必然——赌场场景在预告片中已多次出现，老虎机作为最低门槛、最高频次的赌场组件，几乎不可能缺席。

## Overview

Slot machines are the most ubiquitous random-outcome game in casino settings worldwide — extremely simple rules, fast pace, and low per-spin cost make them the most widely accessible component of any casino ecosystem. With *Grand Theft Auto VI* returning to Vice City and expanding casino settings across Leonida, slot machines as a foundational component of the casino game matrix are virtually certain to return. This entry is marked as speculated because Rockstar has already implemented a complete slot machine system in *GTA Online*'s Diamond Casino & Resort update, but has not explicitly shown the gameplay in GTA6 trailers or press materials.

### Series History

Slot machines in the GTA series trace back to the *GTA Online* casino update, with a framework refined over years:

- ***GTA Online* Diamond Casino & Resort update (2019-07)**: Slots debuted as one of three core games, alongside roulette and blackjack, forming the casino game matrix
- **Themed variety**: Multiple themed machines, each with distinct symbol sets and paytables — e.g., Diamond Miner, Evil Tree, Impotent Rage — echoing Rockstar's own IP
- **Betting modes**: Support for 1–5 paylines, letting players adjust per-spin stake by risk preference
- **Payout structure**: Standard 5×3 reel layout, with payouts based on symbol combinations hit on active paylines
- **Jackpot mechanics**: Certain themed machines feature progressive jackpots, with the player pool contributing to a shared prize that pays out large sums on win
- **Quality-of-life**: A "quick spin" mode accelerates pace for high-frequency players

Continuation into GTA6 is nearly certain — casino scenes appear repeatedly in the trailer, and slot machines as the lowest-barrier, highest-frequency casino component are virtually impossible to omit.
